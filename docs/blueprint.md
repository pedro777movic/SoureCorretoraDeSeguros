# **App Name**: Soure Landing Page

## Core Features:

- Sticky Header: A sticky header that becomes semi-transparent on scroll, with logo, navigation, and social icons (WhatsApp, Instagram). Includes a mobile-friendly hamburger menu.
- Hero Section: Full-width section with primary blue background, company name, tagline, descriptive text, and a prominent WhatsApp CTA button.
- Insurance Options Grid: A grid showcasing different insurance types (Auto, Residential, Life, Dental, Business) with icons, titles, descriptions, and "Solicitar cotação agora" CTA buttons. Includes an accordion for "Seguro Auto" with tabs: "Auto Individual", "Auto Frota", and "Auto Moto".
- Why Us Section: A two-column section highlighting key benefits (WhatsApp support, personalized analysis, no bureaucracy, major insurers, transparency) with checkmark icons.
- How It Works Section: A section explaining the 3-step process (Initial Contact, Profile Analysis, Best Recommendation) with numbered cards and a WhatsApp CTA button.
- Trust Section: A section to demonstrate commitment to responsibility and transparency.
- WhatsApp Lead Generation: Multiple prominent WhatsApp CTA buttons throughout the page for lead generation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#3A73A8) for header, hero section, and key sections.
- Secondary color: Darker blue (#2F5E8A) for hover states and accents.
- Muted color: Lighter blue (#5C8FC0) for card backgrounds.
- Text color on blue background: White (#FFFFFF) for contrast and readability.
- Muted text color: Light gray (#F2F2F2).
- CTA color: WhatsApp green (#25D366) for main call-to-action buttons.
- Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for headlines and body text, offering a modern and readable look.
- Headlines: Bold and uppercase (font-bold, uppercase).
- Clean icons from `lucide-react` for insurance types and benefits, conveying trustworthiness and stability.
- Mobile-first, fully responsive layout, with sections stacking vertically on smaller screens.
- Rounded corners and subtle shadows for cards on hover.
- Smooth scrolling for navigation between sections.
